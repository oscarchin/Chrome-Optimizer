Privacy Policy

This notice describes the Privacy Policy that applies to all information collected or submitted when you install or use Chrome Optimizer extension.

USER ACCOUNTS

You are not required to create a user account or provide any personal information in order to use Chrome Optimizer.

COLLECTION OF INFORMATION

Chrome Optimizer locally on your PC collects tab titles and URLs, and optionally screen captures in order to do what it is supposed to do. All this information is stored only locally, on your computer.

The Chrome Optimizer uses Google Analytics to anonymously collect aggregated usage information, like usage of different features of Chrome Optimizer, performance metrics, and code exceptions. All of this information collected in a way that excludes the possibility to associate by us this usage statistics with a concrete user, and in a full accordance of the Google Analytics respective policies. We use this non-identifying, aggregated information to analyze general trends and manner in which Chrome Optimizer is used, to monitor performance and improve the product, or to develop new products and services.

SHARING OF INFORMATION

We do not collect any personal information. We do not share any personally identifiable information.

CONTACT

If you have questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, you can contact us by email at smile.extensions@gmail.com
